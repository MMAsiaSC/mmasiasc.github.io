Welcome to the 1st ACM International Conference on Multimedia in Asia

The first ACM Multimedia Asia, puts together the long-lasting experience of former PCM and ICIMCS, which both have good history as well as attending experiences. It is a newly established international conference to showcase the scientific achievements and industrial innovations in the multimedia field. Its mission is to illuminate the state of the art in multimedia computing by bringing together researchers and practitioners in this field. In 2019, ACM Multimedia Asia will be held in Beijing, China with an extensive program that includes technical sessions covering all aspects of the multimedia field in forms of oral and poster presentations, tutorials, panels, demonstrations, workshops, doctoral symposium and grand challenge competitions.

The ACM Multimedia Asia 2019 Conference will be held at the  China National Convention Center  in Beijing, China from 16-18 December 2019.
