1. M3Oriental: Workshop of Multimodal, Multilingual and Multitask Modeling Technologies for Oriental Languages
Website: https://sites.google.com/view/m3oriental
In recent years, multilingual speech recognition (ASR) and speech translation (ST) have provided effective communication in global business activities. However, the services for low-resource languages, which more than 50% of the global population speaks, have fallen behind. This workshop aims to investigate effective methods to promote research and explore practical ASR/ST systems for low- resourced languages, especially the languages and dialects people speak in Oriental countries (such as Arabic, Turk, India, Bangladesh, Iran, Mongol, ASEAN countries, Chinese dialectal), by sharing knowledge across multiple languages, multiple modalities, and multiple tasks.

2. Workshop on Intelligent Sports Technologies (WIST)
Website: https://sites.google.com/view/wistacmmultimediaasia2023
The Workshop on Intelligent Sports Technologies (WIST) aims to provide a multifaceted platform for researchers, practitioners, technologists, practitioners, coaches, and athletes to explore and discuss the convergence of multimedia, artificial intelligence, and sports science. The workshop seeks to address the innovative intersection of these fields, focusing on the latest advancements, trends, and applications of multimedia technologies in the sports domain.

Organizer:
Yu-Shuen Wang, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
Wen-Chih Peng, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
Jui-Hsien Wang, Adobe Research



3. Workshop on Low-quality Visual Data for Computer Vision and Media
Website: https://sites.google.com/um.edu.my/lqvdw2023/
This workshop aims to gather a group of researchers and practitioners who shares similar technical experiences and interests in low-quality visual data-related topics, e.g., assessment, enhancement, synthesis, and their related applications in various domains. Technical works using diverse technologies and techniques, including conventional, intelligence, or learning-based approaches are all invited for paper submission and to join us in this workshop.

Organizer:
Ching-Chun Huang, Associate Professor, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
Yuen Peng Loh, Lecturer, Multimedia University
Simying Ong, Senior Lecturer, Universiti Malaya
Mei Kuan Lim, Senior Lecturer, Monash University
